## What's Missing — The $599 Secret

vs. MacBook Air M4:

**Critical** 🔴
- No MagSafe → trip over cable, MacBook goes flying
- No keyboard backlight → can't type in dim cafes
- 8GB RAM fixed → memory pressure with 30+ tabs
- No Touch ID ($599 only) → type password every time

**Inconvenient but livable** 🟡
- No Thunderbolt → USB 3 speeds max
- No P3 Wide Color → less vibrant photos
- No True Tone → no auto color temperature
- No Force Touch → simpler trackpad

## Insight

**1. $599 MacBook = Apple's market share play**

Chromebooks owned education while Apple insisted on $999+ Air. Neo says "we have a $600 laptop too." Ecosystem lock-in over margins.

**2. A18 Pro is a recycled chip — that's how $599 works**

Already mass-produced for iPhone 16 Pro. No new M-series design. Near-zero incremental R&D. Apple's $599 secret: "don't design a new chip."

**3. It's not what's missing — it's what remains**

macOS + A18 Pro + 16-hour battery + 1.23 kg. These four alone crush Chromebooks and budget Windows laptops. Apple defined the "minimum viable MacBook."

**4. Korea premium 14–17% — cheaper products get higher markups**

$599 × ₩1,450 = ₩868,550 but actual ₩990,000 (+14%). M5 Pro/Max is 9%. Lower-priced Apple products carry proportionally higher Korea premiums.

**5. The real competitor isn't MacBook Air — it's iPad**

$599 Neo vs $798 iPad Air + Magic Keyboard. Similar price, different OS. Productivity → Neo. Touch + drawing → iPad. Apple competing with itself at this tier is new.
